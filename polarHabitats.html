Polar Habitat

The Polar Habitat contains many animals which is crazy because of the insanly cold climate. Most of the animals that live in Polar habitats have traits that help them survive. The polar habitats are within the artic and antartic

Polar Bear

The Polar Bear is a hypercarnivourus bear which means he eats more than 70% meat. The Polar Bear resides in the artic circle and is the largest extant (still in exsistence) bear, and extant carnivore.

Artic Fox

The Artic Fox is small fox that resides in the artic. The Artic Fox uses it's color of fur as camouflage and to create heat in the cold enviornment. The Artic Fox eats small creatures they can get a hold of.
